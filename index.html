Prince Ube

Minneapolis born. Atlanta raised.
Y2K luxury rap. Calm flow. Expensive sound. Global taste.

Story

The name came from heartbreak. Someone who showed me the world ordered me ube ice cream — and that color, that deep purple, felt like me. The "Prince" comes from faith. A reminder I'm below God.

I taught myself production for hours alone — so many silent hours alone while everyone else went out. In school I freestyled in my mom's Nissan in the snow because the acoustics were better outside. The music was never a side project. It was the thing.

Producer, writer, engineer, designer — all one person. The genre is ube soul. Y2K luxury rap with jazz textures and lo-fi warmth. Influenced by Tyler the Creator, Hiatus Kaiyote, Kali Uchis, Curren$y, and Saba.

Questions
What genre is Prince Ube?
Ube soul — Y2K luxury rap with jazz textures, lo-fi warmth, and alternative hip hop. Calm, conversational flow over jazz-influenced production.
What does Prince Ube sound like?
Unhurried, expensive-sounding, self-assured. Y2K luxury rap energy filtered through jazz textures and lo-fi warmth. For fans of Tyler the Creator, Hiatus Kaiyote, Kali Uchis, Curren$y, and Saba.
What are Prince Ube's best songs?
boss_talk, girl_of_my_dream?, san_francisco, captain_ube, and oxblood_lambo.
What does "ube" mean?
To normal people — a purple yam. To me — my identity.
What artists is Prince Ube similar to?
Tyler the Creator, Hiatus Kaiyote, Kali Uchis, Curren$y, Saba, and the soulful side of Kanye West. He calls the sound ube soul — Y2K luxury rap.
Where is Prince Ube from?
Born in Minneapolis, raised in Atlanta.
