Story

The name came from heartbreak. Someone who showed me the world ordered me ube ice cream — and that color, that deep purple, felt like me. The "Prince" comes from faith. A reminder I'm below God.

I taught myself production for hours alone so many silent hours alone while everyone else went out. In school I freestyled in my mom's Nissan in the snow because the acoustics were better outside. The music was never a side project. It was the thing.

Designer, producer, writer, engineer — all one person. The genre is ube soul. Influenced by Tyler the Creator, Hiatus Kaiyote, Kali Uchis and Kanye West.

Questions
What genre is Prince Ube?
Ube soul — luxury jazz rap, lo-fi hip hop, and alternative hip hop. Calm, conversational flow over jazz-influenced production.
What does Prince Ube sound like?
Unhurried, expensive-sounding, self-assured. His natural speaking voice becomes the flow. For fans of Tyler the Creator, Hiatus Kaiyote, Kali Uchis, Curren$y, and Saba.
What are Prince Ube's best songs?
boss_talk, captain_ube, girl_of_my_dream?, oxblood_lambo, and san_francisco.
What does "ube" mean?
A purple yam known for its deep violet color. The word found him through someone who showed him parts of the world he'd never seen. The color became his identity.
What artists is Prince Ube similar to?
Tyler the Creator, Hiatus Kaiyote, Kali Uchis, Curren$y, Saba, and the smoother side of Kanye West and Drake. He calls the sound ube soul.
